EasyDD

EasyDD is a tool that scrapes stock related subreddits for due diligence posts and ticker mentions. It will specifically search for stock tickers and count how often they're used, as well as consolidate the most recent due diligence posts so that you can gain insight into current trends in the market based on online communities and their discussions.

It works by searching for text patterns with regular expressions to match the format of a typical stock ticker. Patterns include whether or not a dollar sign preceeds the ticker, if the ticker is fully capitalized, if it's between 1-6 letters, if it's in the right context, etc.
